My Work
The Beginning
- The start of my carving journey goes back all the way to when I was a elementary school kid. Back then, I read a blog about rubber carving and was deeply atracted by the intricacy of the artwork, and created my very first carving when I was in 4th grade.
Meaning to Me
- After almost ten years since I started carving, it has been my go-to source whenever I feel stressful. Having the blade trace through the lines and see the imagery in my mind gradually come into the reality refreshes me and cures me from anxiety.
Artwork Explanation
