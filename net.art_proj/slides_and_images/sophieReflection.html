Be Patient

Good things are always waiting ahead, no matter how desperate the present looks. I’ve been upholding such a theory in my head that a person’s life would never be in constant peaks or valleys, but they go up and down, and we just find our way through it.

Never Give Up

I used to soak myself in my failure for long and couldn’t recover from it, now I learned how to stand up from my failure and look into the future and know that if I’m at the bottom of my life at this moment, then the only direction in front of me now is to go up.
