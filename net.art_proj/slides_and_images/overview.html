LOVE Overview

The "Love" by Michael Samyn is a series of 7 stories arranged by html. Love has a total of 49 pages; however it is very hard to get to every page. For almost everypage, there are 5 different directions to navigate. Therefore the user does not have to navigate in a linear path.

"Sade"
The work draws on Samyn's past experiences, including snippets of letters from past relationships.

"The Love List"
A participatory feture where users can enter names and be displayed on the list.

"She Loves Me"
Though users may take a nonlinear path, most pathways begin with this flower

"Romance"
