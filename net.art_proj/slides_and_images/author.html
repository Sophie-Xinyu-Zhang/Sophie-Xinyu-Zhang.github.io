MICHAEL SAMYN

Michael Samyn is a Belgian artist/programmer, who was born in 1968 Belgium.

His quote from an interview: "it was difficult to get a place where you could have your work be stored permanently."

Group Z, Belgium , was a fictional group made from Samyn and his nine artistic alter egos.

In 1999, Michael created work with Auriea Harvey under the name Entropy8Zuper!

He won multiple awards from art competition and was elected first human Mr.Net Art.

Soon, both Michael Samym and Auriea Harvey left net.art and began collaborating together. They both worked for art video games, as they felt the web was becoming too much like a shopping mall.

They co-founded the independent development studio, Tale of Tales.

During a duration of 12 years, they published 8 different games. (The Endless Forest, The Graveyard, The Path, Fatale, Vanitas, Bientot, Luxuria Superbia and Sunset)

Work was featured in several exhibitions at key galleries, museums and articles such as "Hyperallergic" and the "New York Times".
