In the book The Medium is the Message, Marshall McLuhan described how the medium of messages is equally important as the message it contains. Thus, I put a light bulb on this page to illustrate that although a electric current is a medium, it is capable of providing a space in the darkness for people to read and write. Therefore, the nature of medium is more important then the meaning of the message because it enables the creation of messages.
